Story Time

Before we started the Responsive podcast we started the Responsive podcast, the old one. We gave that a good shot but it didn't really stick. But a little while later we decided to give it another shot.

That's when this podcast took off and we really began to enjoy ourselves. We get to engage with the community live during the podcast for questions, and just have a fun time talking tech.

After a short while we got a discord up and running for the podcast community. This would soon have a mix of members who had and hadn't listened to the podcast but also enjoyed tech.

And here we are today! Still kicking strong and with much more than just a podcast and discord, but also this site with its own blog and showcases!

This is all thanks to the amazing community we have, so a massive thanks goes out to all of you!
